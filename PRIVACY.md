# Privacy Policy for Whizzel

**Effective date:** [FILL IN ON PUBLISH]
**Last updated:** [FILL IN ON PUBLISH]

Whizzel ("we", "us", "the app") is an educational quiz app for children ages 4–12, built with child safety and parent control as first principles. This policy explains what information the app handles, how, and why.

If anything here is unclear, email **nirmal8126ai@gmail.com** and we'll respond within 7 business days.

---

## 1. Who owns the account

Only a **parent or legal guardian** creates and owns the account. Children do not sign up themselves and cannot log in directly. A parent adds one or more child profiles inside their own account.

## 2. What we collect

### From the parent (account owner)
- **Email address** — used to sign in and to send you account-related messages.
- **Password** — stored in hashed form by our authentication provider (we never see the plaintext).
- **Timestamps** — when the account was created and last used.

### From each child profile (added by the parent)
- **Display name** — a first name or nickname chosen by the parent. We do not require or ask for a full legal name.
- **Age group** — a broad range (e.g. 4–6, 7–9, 10–12). We do not collect exact date of birth.
- **Quiz activity** — which questions the child answered, scores, badges, and difficulty level. This is used to show progress and award badges.

### What we do **not** collect
- No location data.
- No contacts, photos, microphone audio, or camera images.
- No precise identifiers (no advertising ID, no device fingerprinting).
- No behavioral profiles.
- No data from children outside what the parent voluntarily adds as described above.

## 3. What we do **not** do

- We do **not** show third-party advertising to children.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics or behavioral trackers that profile children.
- We do **not** sell, rent, or share child data with data brokers or advertisers.
- We do **not** allow in-app purchases by children without a parental gate.

## 4. How the data is used

- To let the parent sign in and return to their account.
- To show each child their own progress, scores, and badges.
- To pick quiz difficulty appropriate to the age group the parent selected.
- To improve the app (aggregate, non-identifying usage, e.g. "how many users answered question X incorrectly").

We do not use the data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to running the app.

## 5. Third-party services we use

These are the only external services that receive any of the data above, and only the minimum needed to run the app:

| Service | What it does | What it receives |
|---|---|---|
| Supabase (supabase.com) | Hosts our database and handles parent sign-in | Parent email/password, child profiles, quiz activity |
| Google Gemini API | Generates the pool of quiz questions | **Not child data.** Only internal prompts for question generation. |

Supabase acts as a data processor under our direction. Their privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Google Gemini is used only on our side to produce the question bank. Children's data, scores, and profile information are **not** sent to Gemini.

## 6. COPPA (United States) and similar laws

Whizzel is intended to be usable by children, but the account and all data decisions rest with the parent:

- Parents provide consent inherently by creating the account and adding the child profile.
- We collect the minimum data needed for the app to work (display name, age group, progress).
- Parents can review, edit, or delete child profiles at any time from inside the app.
- If you believe a child has somehow created an account without a parent's consent, email us and we will delete it immediately.

## 7. Data retention and deletion

- Child profiles and their quiz progress are kept while the parent account is active.
- Deleting a child profile from the app removes that child's display name, age group, and progress.
- To delete the entire account (parent email, all child profiles, all history), email **nirmal8126ai@gmail.com** from the registered address. We will complete deletion within 30 days.

## 8. Security

- All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted with HTTPS.
- Database access is protected by row-level security rules — one parent's account cannot read another parent's data.
- We store only what section 2 lists. No sensitive categories (health, financial, biometric) are handled by the app.

No system is perfectly secure; if we learn of a breach affecting your data, we will notify affected users by email.

## 9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under GDPR in the EU/UK or CCPA in California), you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Email **nirmal8126ai@gmail.com** to exercise any of these rights.

## 10. International users

The app is operated from India. If you use it from another country, your information will be transferred to and processed in the countries where our service providers (Supabase, Google) operate.

## 11. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a way that affects what data is collected or how it's used, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify account holders by email before the change takes effect.
